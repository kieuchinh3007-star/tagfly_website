Our story

The origin of TagFly

01 - Foundation

Born from 9 years of Shopify expertise

Born from a parent company with 9 years of expertise in developing e-commerce apps, particularly for Shopify, TagFly is the culmination of our dedication to empowering merchants.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to craft convenient solutions that help merchants thrive with the products they've passionately built.

02 - Mission

Data tracking made simple - and deep

Seeing too many complicated and unreliable analytics tools, we focused on one thing: making data tracking easy but still deep with insights. We wanted to give store owners a simple tool that really shows what's going on with their customers and how their store is doing, all in clear detail.

Now, TagFly stands as a trusted ally for brands worldwide, transforming complex data into clear, actionable insights.

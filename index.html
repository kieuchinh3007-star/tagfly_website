How does TagFly work?
TagFly is a tracking solution that combines client-side and server-side tracking to provide accurate performance analysis of your store without setup hassles or slowing down your page.

Client-side tracking: We integrate directly with Google Tag Manager, reducing the steps needed for setup. Just install the app, and we'll handle the rest.

Server-side tracking: This ensures more accurate data collection even when client-side tracking is blocked by browser adblockers or iOS 14 tracking restrictions. It allows us to capture checkout steps and transactions, which is more challenging to do via Google Tag Manager alone.
How many marketing channels does TagFly support?
Currently, we integrate with 8 marketing destinations, including: Google Analytics 4, Google Ads, Meta, TikTok, Klaviyo, X (Twitter), Pinterest, and Snapchat.

We are continuously working to add more tracking integrations to meet your marketing needs. Stay tuned for updates!
Is TagFly compliant with GDPR / CCPA?
Yes, we have a consent mode feature that integrates with most Shopify CMPs, allowing you to stay compliant with customer privacy regulations without sacrificing data accuracy.
Are there any discounts or free trials available?
Yes, we offer a 3-day free trial for any paid plans. Currently, we do not offer any discounts.
I am using another app for tracking. What should I do when setting up TagFly?
Using two tracking apps for the same pixel simultaneously can lead to duplicate event tracking. To ensure more accurate tracking, you should disable tracking in one app.

However, we highly recommend creating a new developer mode pixel or GA4 property specifically for use with TagFly and keeping them separate from your existing tracking setup.

How does TagFly work?

Client-side tracking: We integrate directly with Google Tag Manager, reducing the steps needed for setup. Just install the app, and we'll handle the rest.
